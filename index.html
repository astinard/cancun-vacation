Decision: All-Inclusive Resort - Cancun/Riviera Maya/Tulum Area

After considering all options, we're focusing on all-inclusive resorts in the Cancun area. This works well for our multigenerational group because:

  • One price covers everything (food, drinks, activities)
  • Easy for elderly family members - everything on-site
  • Activities for all ages (kids clubs, pools, beach, spa)
  • Direct flights available from most cities

Tulum Options

If we want something more boutique/trendy, Tulum is about 1.5 hours south of Cancun airport. Keep in mind:

  • More bohemian, Instagram-worthy vibe
  • Amazing cenotes and ruins nearby
  • Fewer large all-inclusive options
  • More walking on uneven surfaces - may be harder for mobility issues
  • Longer transfer from airport

Tulum may be better as a day trip from a Cancun/Riviera Maya resort.

Important Notes & Next Steps

  • Accessibility: Call each resort directly to confirm specific mobility accommodations (wheelchair access, walk-in showers, etc.)
  • Group booking: Many resorts offer group discounts for 10+ rooms - ask when calling
  • Timing: Late May is slightly cheaper than June; book early for best rates
  • Room configuration: We'll need approximately 7 rooms for 14 people
  • Travel insurance: Recommended for elderly travelers
